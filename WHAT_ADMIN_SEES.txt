================================================================================
EXAMPLES OF WHAT TO ENTER
================================================================================

Example 1: Popular Product
  Units Sold: 1250
  Rating: 4.8
  → Shows: "⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.8 | 1,250 sold"

Example 2: New Product (hide social proof)
  Units Sold: [leave empty]
  Rating: [leave empty]
  → Shows: Nothing (clean look)

Example 3: Moderate Sales
  Units Sold: 150
  Rating: 4.5
  → Shows: "⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.5 | 150 sold"

Example 4: Perfect Rating
  Units Sold: 500
  Rating: 5.0
  → Shows: "⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 5.0 | 500 sold"
